Unpopunhorroropinion is a site where horror lovers can go to catalog their collection and share photos of their collection with other horror lovers. Along with the ability of reviewing your favorite, or most hated horror films in your collection!

Horrorflow

Solo Project

Horrorflow is a clone of Stack Overflow, but with a twist, like most good horror films! Horrorflow is meant to be a place where horror film lovers can go to ask the tough questions. It is an idea that I am sure many film watchers have wondered. Have you ever watched a horror film and wondered 'What the heck was that ending?!? Well Horrorflow aims to be your one-stop-shop to ask the Who What When Why, instead of scouring the internet endlessly for answers!

Live | Repo

Group Project

Deadreads, a goodreads clone, is a social media application that allows users to review horror books, add horror books to bookcrypts along with, view reviews and profiles of other horror book lovers. Deadreads was made using Javascript, Express, and Pug.
